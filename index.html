Logos InkPen 1.0

Professional vector art & design for Macintosh

Logos InkPen, a Vector Art & Design App for macOS 14, 15 and 26! 1.0b9 available for download. Brush tool adds pressure sensitive Apple Pencil support for Luna Display and Apple Sidecar, and introduces Liquid. Built on SwiftUI, Metal and CoreGraphics. Wacom drivers are recommended. InkPen was inspired by Macromedia FreeHand MX and contains many of its core features.
