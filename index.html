Logos InkPen Beta

Vector art & design concept app for macOS

Logos InkPen, a Vector Art & Design App for macOS 14, 15 and 26! 1.0b16 available for download. Added basic PDF and SVG Export, with non-destructive convert text to outlines. Also improves support for PDF gradients, flat colors, and CMYK 4 color process PDFs that can be color separated using Adobe Illustrator or equivalent process. Built on SwiftUI, Metal and CoreGraphics. Wacom drivers are recommended. InkPen was inspired by Macromedia FreeHand MX and contains many of its core features.
