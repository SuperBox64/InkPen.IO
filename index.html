Logos InkPen Beta

Professional Vector Art & Design for Macintosh

Logos InkPen, a Vector Art & Design App for macOS 14, 15 and 26! 1.0b26 available for download. Adjusted fill and stroke opacity responsiveness. One of the most complete builds to date!

InkPen is perfect for creating Xcode interface elements, SpriteKit 2D sprites, Icon Composer SVGs, web graphics, and Ubuntu Linux icons. Built on SwiftUI, Metal and CoreGraphics. InkPen also features pressure sensitive brushes compatible with Luna Display or Apple Sidecar for ApplePencil on iPad. InkPen was inspired by Macromedia FreeHand MX and contains many of its core features.

Logos InkPen was designed by the Man and Machine, Todd Bruss. After years of dealing with Adobe Illustrator's ginormous feature set and gigabytes of disk space, Todd decided it was time to create something new. The approach, a native App designed on Mac 4 Mac. The goal to make Logos InkPen, the most popular 2D design app on the Apple platform. See more from Logos InkPen in 2026.
