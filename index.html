Logos InkPen Beta

Professional Vector Art & Design for Macintosh

Logos InkPen, a Vector Art & Design App for macOS 14, 15 and 26! 1.0b29 available for download.

InkPen is perfect for creating Xcode interface elements, SpriteKit 2D sprites, Icon Composer SVGs, web graphics, and Ubuntu Linux icons. Built on SwiftUI, Metal and CoreGraphics. InkPen also features pressure sensitive brushes compatible with Luna Display or Apple Sidecar for ApplePencil on iPad. InkPen was inspired by Macromedia FreeHand MX and contains many of its core features.

Logos InkPen aims to be the fastest vector app on the planet. It's achieved something truly remarkable taking full advantage of SwiftUI, CoreGraphics and Metal.
